🍪 Import Session Cookies ★ Recommended
Use Cookie-Editor browser extension to import your Facebook session — works on all platforms, no Selenium needed
Step-by-Step Setup Guide
1
Install Cookie-Editor Extension
Install the free Cookie-Editor extension in your browser. Available for Chrome, Firefox, and Edge.
Chrome Web Store ↗   Firefox Add-ons ↗
2
Open Facebook and Log In
Go to facebook.com in your browser and log into your Facebook account normally. Make sure you are fully logged in and can see your feed.
⚠️ Do not log out after this step
3
Open Cookie-Editor on Facebook
While on facebook.com, click the Cookie-Editor icon in your browser toolbar. The extension will open showing all Facebook cookies.
📍 Must be on facebook.com when you click the extension
4
Export Cookies as JSON
In the Cookie-Editor panel, click the Export button at the bottom. Select "Export as JSON". The cookies will be copied to your clipboard automatically.
💡 The JSON is now in your clipboard — ready to paste
5
Paste Below and Import
Paste the copied JSON into the text area below and click Import Cookies. BIRDY-EDWARDS will validate and save your session.
